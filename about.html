Meet Kim

I’m Kim Wood, LMHC, a Florida-based therapist providing trauma-informed, client-centered telehealth counseling for adults seeking clarity, grounding, and emotional wellness.

We’ll create a supportive space to explore your inner world (your strengths, your wounds, your patterns) and move toward meaningful change.

“Alcoves are formed by years of weathering the side of something as strong as rock, creating a nook of shelter. Whatever your weather, my hope for Alcove is that it becomes a small place of rest and healing, even in the midst of grief, pain, or uncertainty.”
