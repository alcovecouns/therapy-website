About Me

I’m Kim (Kimberly) Wood, LMHC, a licensed mental health counselor in Florida. My work is grounded in compassion, curiosity, and the belief that every person deserves a space where they can feel seen, understood, and safe.

I bring a warm, steady, and client-centered presence to therapy, integrating approaches like EMDR, IFS-informed parts work, DBT skills, attachment theory, and trauma-focused CBT. My goal is to support you in healing patterns that no longer serve you and rediscovering your inner wisdom.

Outside of therapy, I’m someone who values quiet moments, meaningful conversations, and the process of ongoing personal growth.
