My Therapeutic Approach

I work with adults navigating trauma, anxiety, emotional overwhelm, relationship patterns, self-worth, and life transitions. Therapy with me is collaborative and grounded in curiosity—your experiences and inner world guide the process.

Modalities I Integrate

  • Client-Centered Therapy — a warm, validating space centered on you.
  • EMDR — processing trauma and stuck memories to restore balance.
  • IFS-Informed Therapy — understanding parts of self with compassion.
  • DBT Skills — grounding, emotion regulation, and distress tolerance.
  • Attachment-Based Approaches — exploring relational patterns and needs.
  • Trauma-Focused CBT — structured support to shift unhelpful beliefs.

What Therapy Feels Like

Sessions are gentle, thoughtful, and paced with care. We focus not only on challenges but also on resilience, agency, and the deeper roots of your emotional experiences.
